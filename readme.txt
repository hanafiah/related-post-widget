== Installation ==

**Install like any other basic plugin:**

1.	Copy the related-post-widget folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder

2.	Activate the Related post widget on your plugin-page.

3.	Drag the Related post widget to your sidebar and change your own setting accordingly
